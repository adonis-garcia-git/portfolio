What is GLASS?

GLASS (Global Leaders and Scholars in STEM) is an honors program at NYU Tandon that transforms engineering students into globally-minded changemakers. Through the program's five core windows‚ÄîGlobal Competency, Commitment to Service, Leadership, Academic Excellence, and Professional Development‚Äîscholars gain the skills, experiences, and support to tackle real-world challenges while building meaningful careers in STEM.

What sets GLASS apart is the combination of financial support, global opportunities, and a tight-knit community. Scholars receive funding for conferences, study abroad experiences, and professional development. But more than that, you become part of a family of driven students who push each other to think bigger and aim higher.
